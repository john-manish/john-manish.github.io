# MY SCHOOL LIFE COMES TO AN END



School life ends here… Finally the day came,    When all students have bags on their shoulder

But now the feeling of holding the bag is different. Before this period of time……………… Taking school bag to home felt like finish for today..



These friends, those buildings, that strict teacher and those hindering —
I don’t know whether we will be in contact or no..
I don’t Even Know How much Closer i Was with my Classmates but I am going to miss them for sure !
No matter How My ties Was With My Classmates But All Of You Be happy wherever you are, achieve the heights and move on, with your life !!!



But after some years  when we will cross this buildings
Everyone will memorize small, pretty , bad moments and a smile will surely come on face, just wish you all a great life ahead and a urge to respect all The Classmates if You meet By Chance...



Money Can buy anything but You can't buy memories. You make them.
Some memories can never be erased ... How hard you try !!!
Sometime Happy Memories Hurt the Most...



No matter what going on in your life, school memories always makes you happy  !!
