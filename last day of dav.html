# MY LAST DAY OF NS DAV



Maybe It was a month Of Feb/March , 2021. I was in the Session 2020-21. It was tha last Day Of My school Life...It was Practical That Day..
During the time of Covid, all the students came to class after a long period...



I still remember that day , Rohit and I were sitting together on the 4th/5th bench of the 3rd row.
And Shruti was sitting in front of me and Rivesh or maybe Aryan was sitting behind.



And as far as I remember that day, Me,Rohit,Mayank,Utsav and other students tore the graph paper of everyone's practical copy with the permission of some students and without the permission of some students...



That day I knew that it was my last day of that school but I thought that nothing is going to happen now because I will not remember this school anymore. As I had no deep connection with that school..



Now I come to know that how wrong I was !
No matter how Much famous, big, small school you are studying in, but that school life leaves those beautiful memories which you can never erase !



Beacuse You are Not Going to create School Life memories Again...
